Welcome to the WTW Sanctions Process

Willis Towers Watson is committed to complying with applicable sanctions and export and trade control laws and regulations. Colleagues are required to ‘Tell the Sanctions Team’ before performing any activity involving:

  • Touchpoint with a Sanctioned Country
  • Touchpoint with Controlled Goods

and only proceed if approved by the Sanctions Team.You can Tell the Sanctions Team by completing and submitting the relevant Sanctions or Controlled Goods Form.

There are five forms found in the side bar:

  • Consultancy, Advisory or Investment Services Form
  • Insurance or Reinsurance Broking Form
  • Iran Transactions Form
  • Controlled Goods Form
  • Payments Form    

Complete the appropriate form as fully and accurately as possible, providing full details in the form will assist the Sanctions Team to handle your referral quickly.
